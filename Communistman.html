The Communist Manifesto

Written by Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels in 1848, The Communist Manifesto is one of the most influential political documents in history.

“Workers of the world unite; you have nothing to lose but your chains.”

The manifesto outlines the theory of class struggle and the problems of capitalism, and calls for a revolutionary movement towards communism.
